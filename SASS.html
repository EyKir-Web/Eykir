About Our School

Our History

Founded in 1998, Seden Adea Secondary School has grown from a small community school to one of the region's most respected educational institutions. Our commitment to academic excellence and character development has remained unchanged throughout our journey.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential and become responsible citizens.

Vision: To be a center of excellence in education, recognized for developing innovative leaders and lifelong learners.

Principal's Message

"At Seden Adea, we believe every student has unique talents waiting to be discovered. Our dedicated staff works tirelessly to create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally."

Admissions

Requirements

For Grade 9 Entry:

  • Completion of Grade 8 with minimum 2.5 GPA
  • Passing score on entrance examination
  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate and previous school records

For Transfer Students:

  • Official transcript from previous school
  • Letter of recommendation
  • Interview with admissions committee
